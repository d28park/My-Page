The Story

Perhaps you can relate. As my graduation approached, I came to two conclusions:

  1. I don't think I want to pursue a career in my field of study
  2. Previous internships were fun, but I couldn't justify returning to any of them as a FTE

It took a whole undergraduate degree to figure it out, but I believe that my dream job lies within the technology industry. I love the idea of designing, creating, and shipping new goods or services. To be more specific, software is currently the key for ultra-quick development and the possibilities seem endless.

In the last month of 2014, I went from knowing nothing about coding, to releasing a simple game on Google Play. It was a fulfilling moment and I finally started to see direction in my future. Follow me as I absorb knowledge at an alarming rate and work on a variety of projects (like this website!).

  2. Might like Software
  3. Use mighty Internet to learn
  4. Pretty sure I like it
